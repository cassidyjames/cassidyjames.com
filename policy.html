Clean, Cheap, Renewable Energy

Energy affects the price of everything. When gas prices go up, so do bread prices.

A transition from fossil fuels is necessary to combat the largest global security issue of our time: climate change.

Energy production should not be entangled with foreign policy. Domestic clean energy means less global conflict

Universal Basic Income

Means testing is inefficient. Current state assistance programs create a disincentive to find work. UBI means a smaller government that provides a springboard for finding meaningful employment.

UBI allows us to abolish the minimum wage. A free-er market.

Allows for an economic model that doesn't require employment. We shouldn't fight job automation. Our economy should encourage and reward it.

Disincentives wasteful government spending. Creating "jobs" is no longer a concern; doing real meaningful work is.

Criminal Justice Reform

The current criminal justice system unfairly penalizes the poor. Individuals with criminal records have a difficult time re-integrating.

In an ideal society we don't just punish criminals, we seek to remove the conditions that lead to crime.

It should be easier to do the right thing than to do the wrong thing. If it's easier to succeed by harming others, something is broken
